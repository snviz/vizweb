User-centered interactive graphical representations are generated for the purpose of understanding user preferences for visualisations of a behaviour ontology by solving visual tasks with semantics

Heatmap

A two-dimensional plot that shows the strength of relationship between two behaviours. The varying intensity of the color-coded matrix represents the measure of correlation.

• Relational semantics visualization


Arc Diagram

2-D network graph in which the vertices of the graph are placed along a line in the Euclidean plane, with edges being drawn as convex curves. It constitutes of nodes that represent entities and exhibits relationships between entities by links.
Nodes are displayed along a single axis and links are represented with arcs depicting the relationship between the behaviours w.r.t the degree of the nodes.

Chord Diagram

Displaying a tree structure of related behaviours that expands outwards, radially displayed on a polar coordinate system. It is used to show comparisons among the behaviours in a circular shape.

Viz5

Network

Visualise the relationship between behaviours in an interactive, network diagram format where the edges represents the strength of association and the degree of a node is the number of edges that are incident to the node. This allows the users to see how the relationships between behaviours are structured and identify core behaviours whithin the network .
